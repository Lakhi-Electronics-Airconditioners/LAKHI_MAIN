How to link your CustomerCode to your Account

  1. login in to your account if you don't have one signup 
  2. after sign in/up go to your Account dashboard and click change account details (you will see a form same as a the one you ask to fill when you signup)
  3. in the form you will see an option to enter your CustomerCode (enter your CustomerCode) if you don't know your CustomerCode fill out a form to find your CustomerCode 
  4. click save

now, you will see your bills and Customer Info in the account dashboard
